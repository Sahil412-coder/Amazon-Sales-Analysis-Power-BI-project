📊 Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

The Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using Power BI to analyze Amazon product sales data. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, product categories, customer reviews, and revenue trends. It enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify top-performing products, and make data-driven business decisions through interactive visualizations.

🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze Amazon product sales performance.
- Monitor Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales.
- Track monthly and weekly sales trends.
- Identify the best-selling products and categories.
- Compare products based on customer reviews.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Microsoft Excel (Dataset)
- Data Visualization

 📊 Dashboard Features

1. KPI Cards
Displays important business metrics such as:
- Year-to-Date Sales
- Quarter-to-Date Sales
- Total Products Sold
- Total Customer Reviews

2. Monthly Sales Trend
- Shows sales performance from January to December.
- Helps identify seasonal trends and peak sales periods.

3. Weekly Revenue Analysis
- Displays revenue generated each week.
- Helps monitor weekly business performance.

4. Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Compares total sales across different product categories.
- Displays each category's contribution to overall sales.

5. Top 5 Products by Sales
- Highlights the highest revenue-generating products.
- Helps identify best-selling products.

6. Top 5 Products by Reviews
- Displays products with the highest customer engagement.
- Useful for understanding customer preferences.

7. Interactive Filters
  Users can filter the dashboard by:
- Product Category
- Quarter (QTR)

📌 Business Insights

The dashboard provides several valuable insights:

- Camera products generated the highest sales revenue.
- Monthly sales increased significantly during the last quarter of the year.
- Weekly revenue analysis helps identify high-performing sales weeks.
- Some products receive a large number of customer reviews despite lower sales, indicating strong customer engagement.
- Interactive filters allow users to analyze specific categories and quarters efficiently.
